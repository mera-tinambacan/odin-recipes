CORNED BEEF GUISADO WITH POTATO: A FLAVORFUL DELIGHT WITH SURPRISING HEALTH BENEFITS

Beef Guisado with Potato is a delicious and satisfying dish that combines the savory flavors of corned beef and the comforting goodness of potatoes. This delightful Filipino-style recipe is beloved for its simplicity, versatility, and mouthwatering taste.

Ingredients:

  • 2 150g Purefoods Corned beef
  • 1 med. Potato peeled & diced
  • 3 tbsp. Canola Oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic minced
  • 1 med. Onion sliced
  • A pinch Salt & Pepper to taste

Procedures:

  1. Heat 3 tbsp. Canola Oil in a pan.
  2. Add into the pan 1 med. Potato.
  3. A pinch of Salt & Pepper, to taste.
  4. Fry potatoes until lightly brown & drain in paper towel.
  5. Set aside.
  6. Heat 1 tbsp. Canola Oil.
  7. 2 cloves Garlic, minced 1 med. Onion, sliced.
  8. Saute until fragrant & softened.
  9. Add 2 (150g) Chili Garlic Corned Beef in the pan.
  10. Saute for about 7-8 minutes.
  11. Add fried potatoes and saute for another 3-4 mins.
  12. Our Fried Corned Beef with Potatoes is ready to serve!
  13. Serve it with fried rice or hash browns.
