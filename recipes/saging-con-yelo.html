HOW TO PREPARE SAGING CON YELO (SABA CON HIELO) COOLERS

Isn’t it satisfying to make a delicious dessert snack when all you had to do is mix or layer some ingredients? A refreshing drink easy to prepare to fight the summer heat or in any hot weather is a tasty Saging Con Yelo.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups shaved ice
  • 2 cups minatamis na saging with sago
  • ¾ cup evaporated milk, chilled
  • 3 pcs pandan leaves to decorate (optional)

Instructions:

Prepare Minatamis na Saging:

  • Visit our article about Minatamis na Saging Recipe.

Prepare Shaved Ice:

  • Put ice cubes in a heavy-duty blender or ice crusher and turn it on.
  • If using a blender, press the crush button until cubes are crushed, and if you’re using an ice crusher, put ice cubes and press the handle down to shave the ice cubes.
  • Repeat the process until you have the amount of shaved ice needed.
  • Use shaved ice immediately or put it in a freezer while preparing your condiments.

Let’s Assemble our Saba con Yelo:

  • First, we need to prepare our pandan leaves—cut, trim, and arrange them in a serving glass.
  • Put a half cup of shaved ice at the bottom, press lightly, and top with minatamis na saging.
  • Cover with another layer of shaved ice and top with minatamis na saging for the last time.
  • Pour evaporated milk on top and serve immediately.
